Hoda Zamani

Affiliations

I graduated with a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics with summa cum laude level in September 2020. I am currently a senior researcher at the big data research center in Iran working in artificial intelligence algorithms, deep learning algorithms, and data science. Moreover, I’m a manager director of the USERN-IBDAM research group for developing intelligent big data analytics methods in medicine. I published many papers in prestigious journals and conferences and received several awards and honors from the university, IFAC, congress, IEEE, and inventor and innovation award. I have experience carrying out medical data management analysis, machine learning research, and completing relevant projects. I'm interested in researching toward a deeper understanding and analyzing of data and proposing effective optimizers and machine learning algorithms that can extract meaningful insights from various applications.
